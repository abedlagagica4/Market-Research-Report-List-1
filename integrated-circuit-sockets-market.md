<p><p>The integrated circuit sockets market comprises various types, including Dual In-Line Memory Module (DIMM) sockets, production sockets, and test/burn-in sockets. DIMM sockets are designed for installing memory modules in computers, ensuring secure connections for efficient data transfer. Production sockets are used in manufacturing processes, allowing for quick and reliable assembly of integrated circuits. Test/burn-in sockets facilitate the evaluation and reliability testing of ICs by providing temporary connections, ensuring devices perform under stress conditions before final deployment.</p></p>
