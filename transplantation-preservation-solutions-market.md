<p><p>Transplantation preservation solutions encompass various methods to maintain organ viability before transplantation. Static cold storage involves cooling organs in preservation solutions, minimizing metabolic activity. Hypothermic perfusion preservation enhances nutrient delivery and waste removal through continuous perfusion at low temperatures, prolonging organ lifespan. Other methods include machine preservation and normothermic approaches, which utilize controlled environments to optimize cell function and extend preservation time. Each type aims to enhance transplant outcomes by effectively maintaining organ integrity and functionality.</p></p>
