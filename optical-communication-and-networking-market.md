<p><p>The optical communication and networking market encompasses various components essential for transmitting data using light. Optical fibers serve as the medium for signal transmission, while optical transceivers convert electrical signals to optical and vice versa. Optical amplifiers boost signal strength over long distances, and optical switches enable dynamic routing of signals within networks. Optical splitters distribute optical signals to multiple paths, while optical circulators direct light in a single direction, enhancing overall network efficiency and performance.</p></p>
