Wheel-type systems use resilient wheels for smooth movement and are ideal for lighter items. Roller-type systems utilize powered or non-powered rollers for heavier loads, ensuring efficient flow. Conveyor-type systems integrate advanced conveyor mechanisms for continuous movement and sorting of pallets.
