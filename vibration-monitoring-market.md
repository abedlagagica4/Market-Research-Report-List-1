<p><p>The vibration monitoring market is divided into two main types: online and offline vibration monitoring. Online vibration monitoring involves continuous real-time data collection using permanently installed sensors, allowing for immediate detection of anomalies and proactive maintenance. In contrast, offline vibration monitoring includes periodic data collection using handheld devices or portable equipment, which analyzes equipment health during scheduled inspections. Both methods aim to enhance asset reliability, prevent failures, and optimize maintenance strategies in various industrial applications.</p></p>
