<p><strong>The Packaging Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Polypropylene (PP)</li><li>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET)</li><li>Polystyrene (PS) & Expanded Polystyrene (EPS)</li><li>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging resins market consists of several key types: Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and resistant to moisture, ideal for bags and films. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers strength and rigidity, commonly used in containers and bottles. Polypropylene (PP) is durable and heat-resistant, utilized in diverse applications from packaging to automotive parts. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is lightweight and recyclable, ideal for beverage bottles. Polystyrene (PS) is inexpensive and commonly used for disposable items, while Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) provides thermal insulation. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is versatile for pipes and packaging, known for its durability and chemical resistance.</p></p>
