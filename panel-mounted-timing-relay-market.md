<p><p>The panel-mounted timing relay market is categorized primarily into three types: on-delay, off-delay, and others. On-delay relays activate after a preset time delay, allowing devices to start in a staggered fashion, preventing system overloads. Off-delay relays, conversely, keep devices running for a specified duration after control signals are removed, ensuring smooth shutdowns. Other types may include multi-function relays, which offer various timing settings, catering to diverse industrial applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs in automation systems.</p></p>
