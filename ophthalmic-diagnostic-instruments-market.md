<p><p>The ophthalmic diagnostic instruments market encompasses various technologies used for eye examination and diagnosis. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) scanners provide cross-sectional images of the retina. Fundus cameras capture detailed images of the interior surface of the eye. Perimeters analyze visual fields, detecting vision loss. Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems use sound waves for deeper insights into eye structures, while ultrasound biomicroscopes offer high-resolution images of anterior segments. Pachymeters measure corneal thickness, and pupillometry assesses pupil response. Other instruments include tonometers and tear breakup time analyzers.</p></p>
