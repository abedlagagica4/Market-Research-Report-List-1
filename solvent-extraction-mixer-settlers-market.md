<p><p>In the solvent extraction mixer-settler market, there are two primary types: stand-alone and in-column systems. Stand-alone type mixer-settlers operate independently, featuring separate stages for mixing and settling, which allows for efficient handling of large volumes and is flexible for various applications. In contrast, in-column type systems integrate the mixing and settling processes within a single vertical unit, optimizing space and facilitating continuous operation. Both types are essential for efficient separation and recovery in chemical processing and hydrometallurgy.</p></p>
