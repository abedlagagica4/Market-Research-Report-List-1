<p><p>The hand juicers market encompasses two main types: vertical auger models and horizontal auger models. Vertical auger models feature a vertically oriented spiral system that efficiently extracts juice by crushing and pressing fruits and vegetables, optimizing space and minimizing waste. In contrast, horizontal auger models utilize a horizontally aligned auger mechanism, often providing higher juice yields with enhanced versatility for various produce. Both types appeal to health-conscious consumers seeking manual, energy-efficient juicing solutions without relying on electricity.</p></p>
