<p><strong>The SB Latex Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex</li><li>Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SB latex market is categorized primarily into two types: emulsion styrene butadiene latex and solution styrene butadiene latex. Emulsion styrene butadiene latex is produced through a polymerization process in water, resulting in a stable dispersion suitable for adhesives, paints, and coatings. In contrast, solution styrene butadiene latex is created by dissolving polymers in a solvent, making it more suitable for applications requiring high solid content. Both types find widespread use across industries, driven by their versatility and performance characteristics.</p></p>
