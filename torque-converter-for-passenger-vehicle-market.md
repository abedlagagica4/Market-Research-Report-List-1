<p><p>The passenger vehicle torque converter market features two main types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters are designed for simplicity and efficiency, providing basic torque multiplication for smoother acceleration. They are commonly found in standard automatic transmissions. In contrast, multistage torque converters consist of multiple sections that enhance performance, allowing for greater torque multiplication and improved fuel efficiency. These are typically used in high-performance vehicles, offering better power delivery and adaptability to varying driving conditions.</p></p>
