<p><ul><li>Powdery</li><li>Liquid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortar plasticiser market is broadly categorized into powdery and liquid types. Powdery plasticisers are typically dry formulations added to mortar mixes, enhancing workability and reducing water content. They are convenient for storage and transport but may require precise measurement during use. Liquid plasticisers, on the other hand, are premixed solutions that integrate easily into wet mortar mixes, improving flow and adhesion. Both types serve to enhance the performance of mortars in construction applications, offering distinct advantages based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
