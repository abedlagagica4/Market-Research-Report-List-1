<p><p>The UHF RFID inlays market is divided into two main types: UHF dry inlays and UHF wet inlays. UHF dry inlays are typically used in applications where they are directly adhered to surfaces, such as labels or packaging. They require no additional moisture for activation. In contrast, UHF wet inlays are often embedded in materials during production, allowing for seamless integration, especially in more complex applications like apparel tags or access control. Each type serves distinct needs based on application requirements.</p></p>
