<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Distance Measure</li><li>Sonic Distance Measure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distance Measurement Equipment market comprises various technologies, primarily including photoelectric and sonic distance measures. Photoelectric distance measures utilize laser or infrared light to calculate distances by measuring the time it takes for a signal to reflect back, providing high accuracy and precision. In contrast, sonic distance measures use sound waves to determine distance by calculating the time delay of an echo. Both types serve diverse applications, ranging from construction to robotics, enhancing measurement efficiency and reliability in various industries.</p></p>
