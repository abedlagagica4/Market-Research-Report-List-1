<p><p>The 3D white light interferometer market is segmented into single-path and multipath interferometers. Single-path interferometers utilize a single beam of light to capture surface topography with high precision, ideal for simpler applications. In contrast, multipath interferometers employ multiple light paths, enhancing sensitivity and enabling complex measurements over larger areas. This versatility makes multipath interferometers suitable for intricate industrial applications and research, as they provide detailed insights into surface characteristics, making them a preferred choice for advanced metrology needs.</p></p>
