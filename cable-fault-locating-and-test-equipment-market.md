<p><p>Cable fault locating and test equipment includes various specialized tools designed for identifying and diagnosing issues in electrical cables. Cable sheath fault locators detect insulation failures, while cable route tracers help identify cable pathways. Time Domain Reflectometers (TDRs) measure cable length and locate faults by analyzing signal reflections. Pin pointers pinpoint specific fault locations, assisting in precise repairs. Voltage surge generators temporarily send voltage surges through cables to locate faults through the response, enhancing overall cable maintenance and troubleshooting efficiency.</p></p>
