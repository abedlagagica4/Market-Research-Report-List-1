<p><p>The micromanipulator market is segmented into three key types: hydraulic, electric, and manual micromanipulators. Hydraulic micromanipulators utilize fluid power to achieve precise movements and are often favored for their smooth operation and high load capacity. Electric micromanipulators rely on motors for motion control, offering high precision and programmable features, making them suitable for automated tasks. Manual micromanipulators are operated by hand, allowing for direct control and flexibility, often preferred in applications requiring immediate adjustments and tactile feedback.</p></p>
