<p><strong>The Mist Eliminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-mesh Mist Eliminators</li><li>Baffle-type Mist Climinators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mist eliminators are essential components in industrial processes, designed to enhance air quality by removing liquid droplets from gas streams. The wire-mesh mist eliminator utilizes layers of crimped wire to capture and coalesce mist, ideal for lower-pressure applications. In contrast, baffle-type mist eliminators employ a series of baffles to change the direction of the gas flow, effectively trapping larger droplets. Both types are crucial for improving efficiency, reducing emissions, and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations across various industries.</p></p>
