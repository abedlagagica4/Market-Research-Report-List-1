<p><p>The Aluminium Thermal Spray Coating market comprises several technologies. Cold Spray Technology involves accelerating aluminum particles at high speeds to bond them to a substrate without melting, ensuring minimal thermal deformation. Flame Thermal Spray Technology uses a combustible gas to melt aluminum and propel it onto a surface, creating a strong bond. Plasma Thermal Spraying Technology employs high-temperature plasma to atomize aluminum for application. Arc Spraying Technology uses an electric arc to melt aluminum wires, allowing for thick coatings. Other methods may include hybrid techniques or emerging technologies.</p></p>
