<p><strong>The Underground Utility Cable Locators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Frequency Locators</li><li>Multiple Frequency Locators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground utility cable locators market is primarily divided into single frequency locators and multiple frequency locators. Single frequency locators operate at a single electromagnetic frequency, making them suitable for basic applications like locating standard utility lines. In contrast, multiple frequency locators can transmit and receive at various frequencies, allowing for more precise identification of different utilities, depth measurement, and enhanced signal clarity. This versatility makes multiple frequency locators preferable for complex utility mapping and troubleshooting tasks.</p></p>
