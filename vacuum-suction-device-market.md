<p><strong>The Vacuum Suction Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Jet Pump</li><li>Dry Vacuum Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum suction device market includes various types, such as steam jet pumps and dry vacuum pumps. Steam jet pumps utilize high-pressure steam to create a vacuum by entraining fluid, making them ideal for applications like distillation and process industries. Conversely, dry vacuum pumps eliminate the need for liquid, employing mechanisms like mechanical displacement or molecular processes to achieve a vacuum. These pumps are favored in environments requiring clean, oil-free operation, such as semiconductor manufacturing and laboratory applications.</p></p>
