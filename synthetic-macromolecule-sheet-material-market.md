<p><p>The synthetic macromolecule sheet material market encompasses various types like TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and PME-EVA (Polymer Modified Elastomer - Ethylene Vinyl Acetate). TPO is known for its flexibility and UV resistance, making it ideal for roofing systems. HDPE offers high durability and impact resistance, suited for containers and geomembranes. PME-EVA combines flexibility with enhanced adhesion properties, used in applications requiring strong bonding and weather resistance, such as films and coatings in packaging and construction.</p></p>
