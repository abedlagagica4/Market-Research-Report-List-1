<p><strong>The Computer Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CRT</li><li>LCD</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer monitor market is primarily classified into three types: CRT, LCD, and LED. CRT monitors use cathode ray tube technology, offering good color reproduction but are bulky and energy-intensive. LCD monitors, utilizing liquid crystal display technology, are slimmer, lightweight, and more energy-efficient, making them popular for general use. LED monitors, which are essentially advanced LCDs with LED backlighting, provide better brightness, contrast, and energy efficiency, appealing to gamers and professionals who require superior visual quality.</p></p>
