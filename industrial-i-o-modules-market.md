The PLC I/O modules interface discrete or analog signals with programmable logic controllers, enhancing flexibility and scalability. DCS I/O modules are designed for distributed control systems, providing robust solutions for complex industrial processes, often in real-time applications. PC-based I/O modules integrate with personal computers, offering high-speed data processing and advanced control functionalities, making them suitable for diverse automation tasks in varying industries.</p></p>
