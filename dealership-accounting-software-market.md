<p><p>Dealership accounting software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions offer flexibility and accessibility, allowing users to access financial data from any device with internet connectivity. They often feature automatic updates and scalability, catering to the needs of growing dealerships. On-premises software, in contrast, is installed locally on a dealership's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring a higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different operational preferences.</p></p>
