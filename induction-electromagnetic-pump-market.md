<p><p>Induction electromagnetic pumps are categorized into single-phase and three-phase types, depending on the electrical supply. Single-phase pumps require a single-phase power source, making them suitable for smaller applications and residential use. In contrast, three-phase pumps utilize three-phase power, offering higher efficiency and power, ideal for industrial and large-scale applications. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements, such as flow rate, power supply availability, and operational scale, influencing their market positioning and applications.</p></p>
