<p><p>The laboratory VIS spectrophotometer market comprises primarily two types: UV-VIS spectrophotometers and visible-only spectrophotometers. UV-VIS spectrophotometers can measure absorbance and transmittance across a broad spectrum, including ultraviolet and visible light, making them suitable for a wide range of applications in chemistry, biology, and environmental analysis. Visible-only spectrophotometers, on the other hand, focus solely on the visible spectrum, targeting specific applications where UV light is not required, such as colorimetric analysis and educational purposes.</p></p>
