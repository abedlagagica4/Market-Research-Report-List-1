<p><p>The electrically powered spacecraft propulsion market includes several types of technologies. Hall Effect Thrusters (HET) utilize magnetic fields to accelerate ions, providing efficient thrust for long-duration space missions. Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT) generate thrust by expelling ionized gas in short bursts, known for their simplicity and low power requirements. Other propulsion methods may include ion engines and microwave electrothermal thrusters, offering varied performance metrics and applications. Each technology plays a crucial role in advancing space exploration and satellite operations.</p></p>
