<p><p>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF) 3D printers utilize heat to fuse powdered materials layer by layer. The types include Laser PBF, which employs high-powered lasers to selectively melt powders, providing precision and detail for various materials. Electron Beam PBF uses electron beams in a vacuum to achieve similar results, often for metals, enabling faster build times and improved mechanical properties. Plasma Arc PBF utilizes plasma to melt powders, offering versatility in material use and enhanced build quality, suitable for industrial applications.</p></p>
