<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cloud Computing Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Software as a Service (SaaS)</li><li>Platform as a Service (PaaS)</li><li>Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)</li><li>Everything as a Service (XaaS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud computing services are categorized into various market types. Software as a Service (SaaS) delivers applications over the internet, enabling users to access software without installation. Platform as a Service (PaaS) provides a framework for developers to build, test, and deploy applications without managing underlying infrastructure. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) delivers virtualized computing resources, allowing businesses to scale their IT resources on-demand. Everything as a Service (XaaS) encompasses all cloud services, combining multiple offerings into a flexible, on-demand model.</p></p>
