<p><strong>The Immunochemistry Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Antibody</li><li>Secondary Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochemistry reagents market is primarily categorized into primary and secondary antibodies. Primary antibodies are designed to bind specifically to target antigens, initiating the detection process in various assays like ELISA and Western blotting. Secondary antibodies, on the other hand, are raised against primary antibodies and are typically conjugated to a reporter enzyme or fluorophore, enhancing signal amplification and detection sensitivity. Together, these antibodies play a crucial role in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic applications within immunology and biochemistry.</p></p>
