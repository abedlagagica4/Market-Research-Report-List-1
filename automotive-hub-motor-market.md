<p><strong>The Automotive Hub Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hub motor market consists of two primary types: inner-rotor motors and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors feature the rotor located inside the stator, allowing for a compact design and efficient heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, external-rotor motors have their rotor on the outside, resulting in a larger size but providing better torque at lower speeds, ideal for urban commuting. Both types contribute to improved vehicle efficiency and performance, especially in electric vehicles.</p></p>
