The market is segmented into two types: Two-Layer Sheet and Single-Layer Sheet. Two-Layer Sheet tanks feature a glass coating applied to both sides of the steel, enhancing durability and resistance to harsh environments. In contrast, Single-Layer Sheet tanks have glass coating on one side only, providing a more cost-effective option while still offering good performance for various applications.</p></p>
