<p><p>The Hepatitis rapid testing market features two main types: test strips and cassettes. Test strips are simple, portable devices that provide quick results by applying a sample to a reactive strip, making them user-friendly for home or field testing. Cassettes, on the other hand, are compact, enclosed units offering a more controlled testing environment. They often include built-in features to enhance accuracy and ease of interpretation. Both formats enable timely diagnosis, crucial for effective patient management and treatment initiation.</p></p>
