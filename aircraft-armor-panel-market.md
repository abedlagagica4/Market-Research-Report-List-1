<p><ul><li>Aluminum Oxides</li><li>Silicon Carbides</li><li>Boron Carbides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft armor panel market comprises three primary types of materials: aluminum oxides, silicon carbides, and boron carbides. Aluminum oxides are lightweight and cost-effective, often used in lower-impact applications. Silicon carbides offer enhanced hardness and durability, making them suitable for medium to high-threat environments. Boron carbides are among the hardest materials, providing superior protection at a reduced weight, ideal for advanced military aircraft. Each type caters to specific defense needs, balancing performance, weight, and cost.</p></p>
