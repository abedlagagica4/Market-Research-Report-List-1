<p><strong>The Broadloom Carpet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cut Pile</li><li>Loop Pile</li><li>Cut and Loop Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The broadloom carpet market encompasses three main types: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile carpets feature fibers that are sheared to create a soft, plush surface, making them ideal for comfort and aesthetics. Loop pile carpets have fibers that are woven into loops, offering durability and resistance to crushing, suited for high-traffic areas. Cut and loop pile combines both techniques, resulting in unique textures and patterns, providing versatility in design while balancing comfort and strength.</p></p>
