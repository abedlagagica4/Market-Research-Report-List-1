<p><p>Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA) is a high-precision technique used to enhance the properties of thin films, primarily in semiconductor manufacturing. The market for ELA can be categorized into two main types: XeCl (308nm) ELA and KrF (248nm) ELA. XeCl ELA is preferred for larger substrate areas and is effective in improving crystallinity in materials like silicon. KrF ELA, with its shorter wavelength, provides higher resolution and is often utilized for applications requiring fine line patterning and high-quality thin films in advanced technologies.</p></p>
