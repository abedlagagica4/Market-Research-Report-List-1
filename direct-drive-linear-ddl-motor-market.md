<p><ul><li>Ironcore Linear Motors</li><li>Ironless Linear Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Drive Linear (DDL) Motor market comprises primarily two types: Ironcore and Ironless Linear Motors. Ironcore Linear Motors utilize a magnetic core to enhance force density and efficiency, making them suitable for applications requiring high power and precision, such as industrial automation. In contrast, Ironless Linear Motors use a non-magnetic design, which reduces cogging and friction, ideal for applications demanding smooth motion and high responsiveness, like robotics and medical equipment. Both types play crucial roles in advancing automation technologies.</p></p>
