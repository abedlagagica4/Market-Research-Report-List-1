<p><p>Rectifier controllers are essential devices in electrical engineering, converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). The market is primarily segmented into two types: Half Wave and Full Wave rectifiers. Half Wave rectifiers only utilize one half of the AC waveform, making them simpler but less efficient. In contrast, Full Wave rectifiers use both halves, resulting in improved efficiency and smoother DC output. Both types cater to various applications, influencing their respective market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>
