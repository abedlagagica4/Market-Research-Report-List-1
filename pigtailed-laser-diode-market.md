<p><p>The pigtailed laser diode market is divided into two primary types: single-mode and multimode pigtailed laser diodes. Single-mode pigtailed laser diodes offer a narrow output beam, ideal for long-distance communication and high-speed data transmission, ensuring minimal signal loss. In contrast, multimode pigtailed laser diodes emit a broader beam, suitable for shorter distances and applications requiring high power, such as in fiber optics and industrial settings. Both types are essential for advanced optical technologies and telecommunications.</p></p>
