<p><ul><li>Patient Cooling Systems</li><li>Patient Warming Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical temperature management market encompasses two primary types: patient cooling systems and patient warming systems. Patient cooling systems are designed to lower body temperature, commonly used in situations like post-cardiac arrest or heatstroke, to preserve neurological function and improve survival rates. Conversely, patient warming systems aim to elevate body temperature, often deployed during surgical procedures or in cases of hypothermia to maintain optimal body functions and prevent complications. Both systems are essential in enhancing patient outcomes through temperature regulation.</p></p>
