<p><p>The paver market is divided into two primary types: mechanical pavers and hydrostatic pavers. Mechanical pavers utilize traditional steel tracks and engines to provide a consistent material flow and are often employed in large-scale construction projects. In contrast, hydrostatic pavers rely on hydraulic systems for smoother operation and better traction, making them suitable for diverse terrains and intricate applications. Both types cater to different project needs, balancing efficiency and precision in asphalt and concrete laying operations.</p></p>
