<p><ul><li>Solution Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) market is primarily categorized into solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves polymerizing monomers in a solvent, resulting in high molecular weight and improved control over the polymer structure, leading to superior properties for automotive and industrial applications. Conversely, suspension polymerization entails dispersing monomers in a liquid medium, producing high-purity rubber with broader particle size distribution. Both methods cater to diverse applications, from roofing to automotive components, each with unique performance characteristics.</p></p>
