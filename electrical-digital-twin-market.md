<p><p>The Electrical Digital Twin market comprises four key types: Parts Twin, which focuses on individual components and their performance metrics; Product Twin, representing complete products and their operational behaviors; Process Twin, which models manufacturing or operational processes to enhance efficiency; and System Twin, integrating multiple components and processes into a cohesive model for overall system performance analysis. Each type serves distinct purposes, enabling enhanced monitoring, predictive maintenance, and optimization within electrical systems and industries.</p></p>
