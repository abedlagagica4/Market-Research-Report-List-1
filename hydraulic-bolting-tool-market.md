<p><p>The hydraulic bolting tool market comprises two main types: wrenches and torque pumps. Hydraulic wrenches are designed to tighten or loosen bolts efficiently using hydraulic pressure, making them ideal for heavy machinery and construction applications. Torque pumps, on the other hand, generate high-pressure hydraulic fluid to drive hydraulic tools, ensuring precise torque settings for various fasteners. Both types enhance operational efficiency, improve safety, and reduce manual labor in assembling and disassembling equipment in industrial settings.</p></p>
