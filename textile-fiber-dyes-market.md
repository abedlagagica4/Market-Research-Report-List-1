<p><p>The textile fiber dyes market comprises various types, including Vinylsulphone Dye (VS), Monochlorotriazine Dye (MCT), and Bi-functional Dyes. VS dyes are known for their high stability and are particularly effective on cellulosic fibers. MCT dyes offer excellent colorfastness and are commonly used for dyeing cotton and viscose. Bi-functional dyes can react with both cellulose and protein fibers, providing versatility across different textiles. Each dye type plays a crucial role in achieving desired colors and performance in the textile industry.</p></p>
