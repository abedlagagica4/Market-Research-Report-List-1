<p><p>Electric order pickers are classified into three market types based on their lifting capabilities: Low Level, Medium Level, and High Level. Low Level pickers are designed for operations up to 10 feet, ideal for picking items on lower shelves. Medium Level pickers can reach heights between 10 to 25 feet, suitable for warehouses with mid-level shelving. High Level pickers extend beyond 25 feet, enabling access to tall racking systems, making them essential for maximizing storage efficiency in larger facilities.</p></p>
