<p><p>The Advanced Power Modules market encompasses Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) and Power Integrated Modules (PIMs). IPMs integrate power devices, drivers, and protection circuits to enhance efficiency and reliability in applications like motor drives and renewable energy systems. PIMs combine power components into a compact package, optimizing performance for power conversion. Both types aim to reduce size, improve thermal management, and increase system reliability, catering to industries such as automotive, industrial automation, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
