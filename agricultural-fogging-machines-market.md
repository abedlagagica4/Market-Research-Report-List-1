<p><p>The agricultural fogging machines market features two primary types: thermal and cold fogging machines. Thermal agricultural fogging machines utilize heat to vaporize insecticides or other chemicals, producing a fine mist that effectively covers large areas for pest control and disease management. In contrast, cold agricultural fogging machines operate at lower temperatures, using high-pressure systems to create a fog without heating the solution. Both types serve essential roles in agriculture by enhancing the application efficiency of pesticides and improving crop protection.</p></p>
