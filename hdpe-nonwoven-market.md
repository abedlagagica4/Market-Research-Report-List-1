<p><p>The HDPE nonwoven market comprises several key types, each utilized for various applications. Spun-bond non-woven fabrics are created by spinning fibers and bonding them through heat, offering durability and versatility. Airlaid non-woven fabrics involve dispersing fibers in air before binding them, resulting in a soft, absorbent material often used in hygiene products. Dry-laid non-woven fabrics are formed by laying dry fibers, providing consistent thickness and strength. Other types may include spunlace and meltblown nonwovens, expanding application possibilities.</p></p>
