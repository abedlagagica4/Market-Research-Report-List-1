<p><ul><li>Preventive Vaccine</li><li>Therapeutic Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infectious vaccines market is broadly categorized into two types: preventive and therapeutic vaccines. Preventive vaccines are designed to protect healthy individuals from infections by stimulating the immune system to recognize and fight pathogens before they cause disease. In contrast, therapeutic vaccines are aimed at treating existing infections or diseases by enhancing the immune response to eliminate the pathogens or infected cells. Both types play crucial roles in public health, reducing morbidity and mortality associated with infectious diseases.</p></p>
